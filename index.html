About Out of Office

We're an 80s, 90s, and 00s rock cover band that's all about getting people dancing and singing along. If it's popular, we'll play it—and we'll play it loud.

Out of Office brings corporate precision, massive energy, and unapologetic fun to every show. We're a fully mixed, scalable band, equally at home in intimate venues or large PA-driven stages—and we show up ready, reliable, and self-contained.
